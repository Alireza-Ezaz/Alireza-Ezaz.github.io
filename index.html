About

Greetings! I'm Alireza Ezaz, a Computer Science graduate from Brock University with a passion for AI4SE (AI for Software Engineering), trace analysis, microservice architectures, and performance engineering. I recently completed my Master's degree with a perfect GPA of 4.0/4.0, working under the supervision of Prof. Naser Ezzati-Jivan and collaborating with Dr. Majid Babaei from McGill University.

Computer Science Graduate & Software Engineer

My research journey has been centered around AI4SE (AI for Software Engineering), where I explore how artificial intelligence can revolutionize software development, testing, and maintenance. My thesis, "TAAF: A Trace Abstraction and Analysis Framework Synergizing Knowledge Graphs and LLMs," represents a significant contribution to this field, combining the power of knowledge graphs with large language models to enhance software trace analysis.

Throughout my academic career, I've had the privilege of working with exceptional researchers and mentors. Under Prof. Naser Ezzati-Jivan's guidance, I've developed expertise in performance engineering and distributed systems analysis. My collaboration with Dr. Majid Babaei from McGill University has enriched my understanding of machine learning applications in software engineering.

Beyond academia, I bring valuable industry experience from my work at Cohere, where I contributed to data quality and AI systems development, and DataAnnotation, where I specialized in AI data annotation and quality assurance. I also have startup experience as a co-founder of Seventask, a task management web application. These experiences have given me a unique perspective on bridging academic research with real-world applications.

I'm actively seeking opportunities to collaborate on cutting-edge research projects, particularly in AI4SE, trace analysis, and performance engineering. Whether it's academic research, industry partnerships, or open-source contributions, I'm excited to work with like-minded professionals who share a passion for advancing software engineering through artificial intelligence.
